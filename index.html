About Ardrobot: Internet Controlled Robots

Do you dream of robots? Of what they could let you accomplish? Or simply how the right bot could give you more free time? This has been a collective dream of the human race in books, movies, and comics for generations. Now the pieces are falling into place to make it happen. We just need to connect the dots.

This is where Ardrobot comes in. We are building a complete system to control robots using off-the-shelf and open-source components. Our goal is to create the easiest, most cost-effective robot platform on earth.

Being internet based, Ardrobot robots can be piloted nearly anywhere in the world. Cloud-based brains allow for rapid improvements through central code updates, shared sensor data, and integration with other internet services.

This is all being done with a system so simple it will allow nearly any machine to be converted into an Ardrobot powered robot for a few hundred dollars.

Get Involved. We need your help.

Are you a software developer, engineer, maker, or just really handy with duct tape? If so, we need your help!

We are currently in our first developer release of the core Ardrobot components. The initial version allows you to spin-up a local Ardrobot server on your computer and control a robot over your local wifi connection. This will let us test and improve the software, as well as make a number of Ardrobot compatible robots, before we roll out on a larger scale. And, since all of the components we are releasing are open source, you can help us make them better.

Check out the Ardrobot Wiki for more information and to find out how you can get involved.
